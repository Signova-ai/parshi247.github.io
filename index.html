Your documents are protected with bank-level 256-bit encryption, SOC 2 compliance, and comprehensive audit trails. We maintain the highest security standards in the industry.

Yes, electronic signatures created with Signova are legally binding and compliant with e-signature laws worldwide, including ESIGN Act, eIDAS, and other international regulations.

Absolutely! Signova integrates with 1000+ popular business tools including Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Drive, Dropbox, and more. We also provide robust API access for custom integrations.
